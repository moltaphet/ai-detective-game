HOW TO PLAY
What is this?

An AI-powered blockchain detective game where every crime scenario is dynamically spawned and enforced by intelligent smart contracts on GenLayer. Each interrogation is a real on-chain transaction validated by a decentralized network of AI nodes.

01
Connect Your Wallet
Click Connect Wallet and approve in MetaMask. Each wallet address maintains its own fully isolated crime vault — no two detectives share history.
02
Review the Case Dossier
Read the active Case Dossier to understand the crime scene and the suspect's profile. Past cases are accessible in the Case Archives Vault below the dossier.
03
Interrogate the Suspect
Type your question and press Ctrl + Enter (or click Interrogate Suspect). Each question is broadcast as a blockchain transaction — no shortcuts, no simulation.
04
Wait for AI Consensus
The decentralized AI validator network analyzes your prompt and reaches consensus on the suspect's testimony. This typically takes 2–5 minutes on StudioNet.
05
Crack the Case
Study the suspect's responses for slips and contradictions. Uncover the hidden Secret Word buried in the evidence trail to solve the case and claim the jackpot.
